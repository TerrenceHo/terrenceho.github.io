A Little About Myself

I am currently a freshman in UCLA with a large interest in data science. I primarily have used Python for data analysis and scraping, although I am looking to expand beyond this limitation. I've also dabbled with other areas of programming, such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript for web development, a little bit of Php for backend, and I've learned Java and C++ in my classes.

Outside of programming, my interests lie in reading books, dreaming up startup ideas, writing short stories, and playing tennis.

Meal Planner App

Created at a UCLA beginner hackathon called Hack on the Hill, this app allows you to put in your favorite foods from the UCLA dining menu and be notified if the meal is present on today's menu. Every day at midnight, the app scrapes the menu to update it's information. I worked mostly on the front end of the app, learning how to display the information corrently with Android elements.

https://github.com/TerrenceHo/B-Faves

Bruin Units

[Currently in progress] Every quarter, Bruins all have trouble enrolling in their classes, wondering if they would get into their preferred class. BruinUnits aims to take out the uncertainty of process by calculating the chance of enrolling in a certain class based off of one's enrollment time.

https://github.com/TerrenceHo/BruinUnits
